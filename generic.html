AI Seeds App

AI Seeds App is my graduation project, developed to support Saudi Arabia’s Vision 2030 goals for sustainability. It combines AI and mobile technology to help farmers and individuals analyze soil conditions and receive tailored planting recommendations using a custom-built Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) with 95% accuracy.

The app features real-time chat, expert advice, and smart irrigation guidance to promote local agriculture, environmental awareness, and sustainable farming practices.
