Heritage Keeper

Flagship Project • Role: Gameplay Programmer • Engine: Unity 6

A first-person puzzle adventure blending environmental storytelling and educational content about Arabian heritage.

Genre Puzzle Adventure
Role Gameplay Programmer
Language C#

Overview

Heritage Keeper invites players to explore ancient chambers and decode forgotten scripts. Educational content is woven into puzzles and environment to create a learning-through-play loop.

My Role

  • Player controller and input integration
  • Puzzle framework (event-driven, modular)
  • Lantern mechanic and lighting integration
  • Audio manager and camera shake/effects
  • System architecture and code reviews

Gameplay

Players navigate, interact with artifacts, solve cipher puzzles, and use a lantern to reveal hidden glyphs. Difficulty scales through puzzle composition and environmental clues.

  • Puzzle flow with states (idle, engaged, solved)
  • Contextual input prompts and accessible interactions
  • Save points with progress serialization

Technical Challenges

  1. Creating a modular puzzle system to support many puzzle types while keeping low coupling.
  2. Optimizing the lantern and lighting (mobile considerations) while preserving atmosphere.
  3. Implementing save/load for nested puzzle states.

Architecture

Key subsystems:

  • Core: GameManager, EventBus, SaveManager
  • Puzzle Framework: IPuzzle interface, PuzzleController, PuzzleFactory
  • Audio: AudioManager with SFX pooling and layered music tracks
  • Input: Unity Input System abstraction for mapping

Educational Vision

Puzzles teach through discovery; narrative nodes give language history context. Designers and educators collaborated to ensure accuracy and accessibility.

Lessons Learned

  • Favor data-driven puzzle definitions to iterate with designers quickly.
  • Profiling lantern shaders early helped avoid mobile bottlenecks.
  • Playtesting revealed the need for progressive hint systems.
